Usage Instructions

To convert text, simply edit the input fields. The conversion process will automatically take place as you type or make changes.

Shortcuts:
- Press Ctrl + Shift + L to toggle between light and dark modes. (Works in the extension popup or new tab)
- Press Ctrl + Shift + O to open the extension in a new tab. (Works in the extension popup only)
